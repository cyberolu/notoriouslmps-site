NotoriousLMPs

Bold handmade lamps with a story.

It starts with a bottle

NotoriousLMPs was born from a love of upcycling, colour, and objects that have lived a life before they became something new.

Each lamp is handmade using carefully selected bottles, paired with bespoke shades to create bold, one of a kind statement pieces. No two are ever the same.

Designed and handmade in Scotland, every piece is created slowly, thoughtfully, and with character. These are not mass produced lights. They are lamps with personality.

When a piece is gone, it is gone. That is part of what makes each NotoriousLMPs lamp special.
